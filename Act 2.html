Project Proposal: RSA Rugby Skills & Fitness Hub

A Web-Based Platform for Learning Rugby and Promoting Health

Proposed by: Botshelo Letebele Thee Software developer

1. Introduction

The RSA Rugby Skills & Fitness Hub is an interactive, mobile-friendly website designed to teach rugby fundamentals, fitness, and healthy living across South Africa. Built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.js, this platform will offer:

  • Free video tutorials (passing, tackling, rules) for beginners.
  • Personalized training plans for school/university players.
  • Rugby-specific gym workouts (strength, agility, injury prevention).
  • Community forums for coaches to share drills and feedback.

2. Significance of the Project

Bridging Gaps in RSA Rugby Development

  • Centralized Learning: Combines scattered resources into one free, ad-free hub.
  • Health Advocacy: Teaches nutrition and safe training practices to reduce injuries.
  • Digital Inclusion: Works on low-end phones, ideal for townships/rural areas.

3. Problem Statement

Key Challenges Addressed

  1. No structured free training - Paid courses dominate the market.
  2. Lack of beginner-friendly content - Rugby rules and techniques are poorly explained.
  3. Limited coach-player interaction - Beginners struggle without feedback.
  4. Mobile-unfriendly resources - Many sites fail on affordable devices.

4. Target Audience (Rugby Lovers)

Group Needs Platform Features

6. Summary

The RSA Rugby Skills & Fitness Hub leverages simple web tech (HTML/JS) and React.js to create an inclusive platform that:

✔ Teaches rugby for free to beginners and schools.
✔ Connects coaches and players via forums.
✔ Promotes fitness and nutrition tailored to rugby.
✔ Runs smoothly on any device, even with slow internet.
